AI Disclosure Decision Matrix

Criterion High (Must Disclose) Medium (Consider Disclosing) Low (May Not Need Disclosure)
Agency AI makes autonomous decisions for the user

Example: AI-driven trading bot that executes trades without user intervention

 AI provides suggestions, but user has final say

Example: AI-powered email response suggestions

 AI assists but doesn't make or suggest decisions

Example: AI-enhanced spell checker in a word processor
Creativity AI generates entire content or makes significant modifications

Example: AI-written news articles or blog posts

 AI augments or partially modifies user-created content

Example: AI-assisted code completion in an IDE

 AI makes minimal or no changes to content

Example: AI-powered image resizing tool
Privacy AI collects or uses extensive personal or sensitive data

Example: AI-powered health diagnosis system using detailed medical records

 AI uses some personal data, mostly non-sensitive

Example: Personalized movie recommendations based on viewing history

 AI uses minimal or no personal data

Example: AI-powered image recognition tool that doesn't store user images
Impact AI could significantly influence opinions or cause harm

Example: AI-curated news feed that could create filter bubbles

 AI has moderate influence or potential for harm

Example: AI-suggested job listings in a job search platform

 AI has minimal influence or potential for harm

Example: AI-powered music recommendation system
Visibility AI's presence and role are not apparent to users

Example: AI-generated responses in a customer service chat, not disclosed as AI

 AI's presence is somewhat apparent or disclosed

Example: AI-powered virtual assistant with partial explanation of its capabilities

 AI's presence and role are clear and obvious to users

Example: Clearly labeled AI chatbot for customer service
Integrity Significant legal or ethical concerns exist

Example: AI system for criminal sentencing recommendations

 Some legal or ethical considerations need addressing

Example: AI-powered resume screening in hiring processes

 Minimal legal or ethical concerns

Example: AI-enhanced search algorithm in a website
